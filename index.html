My Story

Learning to Love Yourself is Everything

Rooted in a proud Rajput heritage from Jaipur, Chitrangana brings an unmistakable blend of regal poise and modern sophistication to every project. From walking the ramps of Lakme Fashion Week to gracing international magazine covers, her journey is one of fierce determination, cultural pride, and effortless grace.

She proves that fashion has no age limit — working exclusively with luxury houses and couture designers to create campaigns that celebrate the timeless beauty of Indian women on the world stage.
